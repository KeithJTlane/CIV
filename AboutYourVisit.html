Here is some useful information about our award winning B&B to help you make the most out of your stay here

Getting to and from Moore Lodge

  • Moore Lodge is 50 miles / 80 Km from Shannon airport and about a 2-hour drive from Dublin airport.
  • The main bus station in Galway City is a 10 minute walk from Moore Lodge
  • Taxis are available from the station
  • A local commuter bus runs from the station every 30 minutes and stops at Lanes newsagents which is 20 metres from Moore Lodge

About your reservation

  • The regular check-in times are daily from 12:00pm – 6pm.
  • Please make the B&B aware if you plan to arrive outside of these hours.
  • Free private off street car park
  • Free 24 hour wireless broadband Internet
  • Rooms can be cleaned each day if requested, please leave all dirty towels on the bathroom floor
  • Laundry services is available for a small fee
  • All taxes are included in the rate.

Breakfasts at Moore Lodge

  • Full Irish Breakfasts are served from 7.30am weekdays and 7am weekends up to 11am
  • Continental Breakfast are available from 07.30am weekdays and 7am weekends up to 12pm
  • Pleae notify us if you have any dietary requirements
  • Breakfast cereals are left out in the breakfast room for self service throughout the morning
  • * Some breakfast cereals contain certain allergens, please notify us in advance if there is a risk to a member of your party

Going out and about in Galway City

  • Taxis are available on request(Please allow 15 minutes for bookings)
  • The nearest bus stop is only 20 metres away and runs every 30 minutes until 10.30pm.
  • Bicycles are available upon request
