Welcome to MooreLodge B&B

Gillian Moore, your host, is the owner of this unique Bed & Breakfast, located on the west coast of Ireland in the heart of Galway city. Gillian’s business is a family run B&B,has been in operation for the past 20 years and offers spectacular views over Galway Bay. Gillian’s business was awarded a TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence in 2015 and 2016

Why would you choose MooreLodge?

MooreLodge is one of just a handful of luxury bed and breakfast operators in Ireland to be granted the a TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence in 2015 and 2016 Located less than 5 KM from Galway city center we have both a central position while also beibg located les than 500 metres from our spectacular coastal roads. The Bed and breakfast offers convenience, value and optimum comfort in this gorgeous destination. This 5 star bed and breakfast enjoys all the benefits you would expect to find in a luxury B&B or hotel, along with the outdoor pleasures of beautiful Galway Bay.
